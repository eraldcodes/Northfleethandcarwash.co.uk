While we specialises in high performance tyres, we also stock tyres for everything from economy hatchbacks to off road 4x4 tyres, track day tyres and light commercial tyres. Stop by our warehouse today or search our entire inventory online via this website, and start saving today!.

We also offer tracking and alignment services to ensure your tyres are running on even treat to maximise efficiency. As part of the service at we balance all wheels using the latest technology, wheels are balanced to the finest tolerances using a selection of different weights depending on the manufacturer’s specification. Balancing tyres correctly is very important to avoid vibration through the steering wheel.
